Coventry Christmas Market 2022

15th November 2022 - 8th January 2023!

Christmas market is returning to city centre, starting from 15th of November all the way to 8th January. During this time the busy streets of Coventry will transform into cosy Christmas flavoured alleys that will be hosting stands offering food, drinks, and sweets for everyone’s taste. Local craftsmen will be presenting their unique handcrafted items, which will make a perfect Christmas gifts for friends or family. find out more...

Skate into Coventry

26th November 2022 - 9th January 2023!

Get your skates ready and prepare to glide into Winter Wonderworld during the Christmas holidays. Coventry Cathedral will be hosting an outdoor ice ring for everyone to enjoy with their friend, family and loved ones. This magical ice-skating experience on the ruins of Coventry Cathedral, with food and drinks, will be a day out full of fun.
Tickets start at just £5 (limited availability), so book early to get the best prices. Warm up with delicious hot drinks and cake before or after your skating session, add it to your booking for just £4.50.
We recommend wearing gloves and thick socks on the ice. Skaters must be 5 years old or older and skaters under 12 must be accompanied by a skating adult.
