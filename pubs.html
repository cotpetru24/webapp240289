Broomfield Tavern

Local Branch Apple Cider of the Year 2022. A real free house, under renovation. The range of beers offers an unusual variety of choices. Friday night music sessions often feature famous musicians. There is also live music on Mondays and Thursdays. This Victorian pub - now a subsidiary of Pub of the Year and West Midlands Regional Cider Pub of The Year - is located in the countryside but just behind the main road near Coventry rugby field.

Golden Cross

Built around 1853, its owners say it's also the most friendly pub in town. You'll enjoy traditional home-cooked British food, real beer from afar and local gin in a historic setting. The pub has many original Tudor features, including jet-rayed upper floors and Dragon Beam that allows jet floors to go around corners. Customers can also enjoy live music events, open mic nights, and more!
