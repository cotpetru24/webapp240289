West Orchards

West Orchards is a staple of Coventry's shopping scene. Conveniently located in the heart of Coventry, it is an easy walk from anywhere in the city centre. With more than 40 stores, including Primark, JD Sports and other high street favorites, the expansive mall is one of the area's most popular shopping therapy spots. The West Orchards Shopping Center spans 5 floors and offers a variety of snacks during your visit. Food court options include Subway, KFC, and a Chinese noodle bar.

Arena Park

Arena Park is a massive shopping centre next to Ricoh Arena, the former home of Coventry's football team. Arena Park is about 3.5 miles from the city centre and just an 8-minute drive or 30-minute bus ride from central Coventry. Arena Park is home to high-street favourite's such as Next and New Look, as well as a branch of Decathlon, the megastore of popular Sports he. There is also Marks & Spencer for a wide range of groceries and Tesco Express for further grocery shopping.
